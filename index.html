Spine Hero

Upgrade your posture - let us take care of your sitting behavior!

Better health = better life!

Wrong sitting in front of a computer causes a lot of problems like back pain, neck pain and headaches.* Spine Hero offers an innovative solution for this area!

It uses webcam and/or depth camera (like Microsoft Kinect sensor) to detect your sitting posture. When you sit incorrectly, it will simply notify you. To bring you the best results, we have developed SpineHero in cooperation with physiotherapists.

Fluent work thanks to smart timing of notifications!

Our goal is to not interrupt you. Notifications are showed only when we are sure that they will not distract you. For example, we will not notify you, when you will write your email.

They are also designed in the way that they let you focus on your important tasks.

93% accuracy of a wrong sitting posture detection

When we were developing Spine Hero, we were inspired by the project Watson from IBM. That's why we use several so-called "watchers" to detect if you are sitting right.

They use different approaches based on depth and color information from your cameras. In our testing, we achieved 93% accuracy1.

1 total results presented at IIT.SRC conference 2015
