Spine Hero

Upgrade your posture - let us take care of your sitting behavior!

Better health = better life!

Wrong sitting behind computer causes many problems like back pain, neck pain and headache.* JuSiRi offers innovative solution for this area!

We use webcam and depth camera (like Kinect) to detect your position. To bring you best results, we have also cooperated with physiotherapists.

Fluent work thanks to noninvasive notifications!

Our goal is to not interrupt you. Notifications are showed only when we are sure, that they will not distract you.

They are also designed in the way, that they let you focus on your work.

90% accuracy of detection of wrong posture

When we were developing JuSiRi, we were inspired by project Watson from IBM. That's why we use several so-called "watchers" to detect, if you are sitting right.

They use different approaches based on depth and color information from your cameras. In our testing, we measured 90% accuracy.
